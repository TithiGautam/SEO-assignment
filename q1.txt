SEO : It is an acronym acronym for Search Engine Optimization.is a set of practices 
aimed at improving the website visibility on search engine like google.
The goal is to enhance the website's ranking in search result for relevant 
queries,ultimately driving more organic traffic.

Importance of SEO :

Importance of SEO lies in its impact on online visibility and traffic.When your
website ranks higher in search results,it is more likely to be seen by users searching 
for related information,product,or services.This increased visibility can lead to higher
click-through rates,more site visits,and potentially more conversion or engagement.
