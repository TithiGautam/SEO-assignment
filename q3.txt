Benifits of using meta tags:

1.Improved search engine ranking :Well optimized meta tags can positively impact 
your website visibility in search engine results,leading to higher rankings.

2.Increased click through rates:Engaging meta titles and description can encourage
users to click on your link ,improving the CTR and driving more traffic to your site.

3.Optimized social media sharing:Some meta tags like open graph tags,impact how content
appears When shared on social media,ensuring a more attractive and informative
presentation.
